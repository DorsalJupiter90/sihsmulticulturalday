What is Multicultural Day?

Multicultural Day is an annual event celebrated at St Ives High School that brings together students, staff, and the wider community to celebrate the cultures of the world

There are cultural performances, food, music, and activities throughout the day as part of celebrations

Multicultural Day has grown to become one of the most anticipated events at our school, fostering cross-cultural friendships, promoting cultural exchange, and encouraging open dialogues about identity and heritage.

Our goal is to cultivate a sense of cultural appreciation and understanding among our students.

Multicultural Day plays a crucial role in giving students the chance to engage with cultures they may not be familiar with, challenging preconceived notions and broadening their perspectives.
