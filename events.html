Food Trucks

Experience a delectable world tour of flavours at our Multicultural Day's food trucks. From savoury Japanese sushi to fiery Mexican tacos and aromatic Indian curries, indulge in a diverse range of global cuisines, celebrating the richness of our community's cultures, all in one place.

Cultural Concert

Join us as we celebrate diversity through the universal language of music, with a special guest performance by none other than one of the most influential artists of all time, Kanye West. Through his music, Kanye addresses cultural and societal issues, sparking conversations that transcend geographical borders. His songs tackle topics like racism, mental health, consumerism, and fame, providing a platform for open dialogue and shared understanding among people from different walks of life.

Cultural Speakers

Meet our inspiring guest speakers, Ben Popham and Mimi Kwa, as they show kids the power of cultural understanding. Through captivating stories, Ben illustrates how diverse traditions create a richer world. Mimi shares her journey, encouraging unity by celebrating differences. Join us for a lesson in harmony and curiosity.
